FIR -- CLASSIC BOARD GAME




Five in a Row, also called Gomoku. It is an abstract strategy board game.

Players alternate turns placing a stone of their color on an empty intersection. The winner is the first player to form an unbroken chain of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Demo

This game is writen by React.js. I used node.js to create the project and deployed on Heruoku. The Component of React is a powerful feature. It allows us to do OOP in front-end part.

Challenges here are being familiar with unique rules and syntax in React, such as Component and State. and how to organize theose different objects.

RAINBOW REEF -- CLASSIC BRICK BREAKER GAME




Rainbow Reef is a classic brick breaker game. Player controls a bar to move horizontally by keyboard, and try to catch and bounce the ball to break the bricks above.

It is multiple levels game, different color of bricks grand you different value. There are also different items that helps you in the game.
